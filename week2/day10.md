# Day 10
## Key Concept
- IaC --- term to describe an infrastructure in the form of code
- playbook --- conditional step to follow, a reusable template, e.g. investigate a data breach, ransomware
- IPv6 --- provide much more ip addresses than IPv4
- IPv6 & IPv4 --- method of communication
    - Tunnel --- encapsulate one protocol within another
    - Dual-stack --- have the option to use both IPv4 and IPv6
    - Translate --- convert between IPv4 and IPv6
